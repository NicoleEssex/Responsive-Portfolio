About

Nicole Essex is from St. George, Utah. She is an '04 Graduate of the University of Utah with a Bachelors of Science, majoring in Political Science. She is currently working toward becoming a Web Developer and will be completing a University of Utah Professional Certificate Program in Web Development this summer, July 2018.

Nicole is looking forward to starting her career as a developer.
